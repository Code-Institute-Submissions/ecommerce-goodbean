Ethiopia Blend

Origin - Tefera Kebede

Nestled in the southern region of Sidamo in the Ethopian highlands the beans from this farm are strictly high grown. The slower growth allows for a distinctive flavour. The result is an apricot, tamarind and sparkling acidic flavour note. Tastes great as a long strong black.
